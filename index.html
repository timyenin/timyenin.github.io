Who I Am

BACKGROUND

I'm a Cybersecurity B.S. student at the University of North Georgia, currently transferring to the Dahlonega campus as I advance through the program. My passion lies in defending the full spectrum of the cyber landscape — from governance, risk, and compliance frameworks to the frontlines of security operations, and into the rapidly evolving threat surface introduced by artificial intelligence and large language models.

MISSION

We are living through a generational shift in how technology shapes society — and with it comes an expanding attack surface that demands skilled, adaptive defenders. My goal is to be one of them. Whether that means building compliance programs, monitoring threats in a SOC, or helping organizations navigate the security implications of AI, I'm committed to developing the skills and experience needed to make meaningful contributions to cybersecurity at every level.

RIGHT NOW

I'm an applicant for UNG's Cyber Leader Development Program (CLDP) at the Institute for Cyber Operations. Outside of class, I'm actively building a hands-on security homelab covering networking, virtualization, DNS filtering, and local AI inference. I passed the CompTIA Tech+ and am pursuing further credentials while preparing for internship and entry-level opportunities in the field.
